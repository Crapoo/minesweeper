The Rules

The rules are simple:

  • Click on a mine, you lose
  • Click on an empty cell, you live (for now)
  • Click on a numbered cell. The number inside the cell is the number of mines located in touching cells.

A little help.

Are you positive a cell is holding a mine? Right click it! It'll put a flag on it and prevent you from misclicking it.

If you put the flag on the wrong cell, no worries. Right click again and get rid of that flag!
